# Project Overview – Campus Marketplace

## Problem Statement
University students often struggle to buy, sell, or trade items such as textbooks, electronics, and furniture within their campus communities. Existing general-purpose marketplaces lack trust mechanisms tailored to students, making transactions risky and inconvenient. Without a secure, campus-specific platform, students face challenges in verifying buyers/sellers, managing safe exchanges, and ensuring reliable payments.

## System Objectives
The Campus Marketplace system aims to:
- Provide a **secure, student-only trading environment** through verified user roles (Student, Trade Facility Staff, Admin).
- Enable **item listing and browsing** with detailed metadata (photos, categories, condition, and price).
- Facilitate **safe exchanges** via managed drop-off and collection slots at campus trade facilities.
- Support **online payments** with the option to supplement shortfalls in cash, tracked through the platform.
- Build **trust and accountability** through ratings, reviews, and moderation of abusive content.
- Deliver **analytics dashboards** to monitor platform usage, trade facility utilisation, and transaction trends.
- Integrate **South African consumer price data** to provide informed item price suggestions.

## Solution Description
The proposed solution is a **web-based peer-to-peer trading platform** designed exclusively for university students. The system will incorporate:

- **User Verification** via a third-party identity provider to ensure only legitimate campus members participate.  
- **Listing Management** for students to create, edit, and manage item postings.  
- **Buyer Experience** with advanced search, filtering, and transaction history visibility.  
- **Trade Facility Management** to coordinate secure drop-off and collection slots, ensuring safe item exchanges.  
- **Payments Integration** with a third-party gateway, supporting both full and partial online payments.  
- **In-App Messaging** for buyers and sellers to negotiate before committing to a transaction.  
- **Analytics Reporting** to track popular categories, facility utilisation, and moderated content.  
- **SA Data Integration** to provide reliable, data-driven price suggestions based on consumer market trends.  

By combining **Agile methodology**, **CI/CD pipelines**, and a **test-driven development approach**, the Campus Marketplace will deliver a scalable, secure, and student-centric solution. The platform not only addresses the immediate need for safe campus trading but also fosters trust, transparency, and efficiency in student-to-student commerce.
